About me

Hey! My name is Ivan Anfalov, and I'm a Game Design student at the University of Europe for Applied Sciences in Berlin. I am a beginner game designer and I am passionate about developing games by combining creativity with my technical skills to build unique experiences. And on this page you can see some of my projects and learn more about me.
